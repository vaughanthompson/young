5 Halcyon, Cashmere


Our clients love mid-century modern architecture, as do we, and this has worked well on this site.

The home is a compact 4 bedroom on a very steep hill site. The MCM style works particularly well in this setting, with expansive glazing capturing views over the city to the Southern Alps.

We've exposed the structure, and combined natural timbers with a colour palette and joinery style reminiscent of classic MCM, working closely with the clients who are passionate about their new home.
