95 Bachelors, Little River


The home in Little River is completely off grid, and self sustaining, generating its own power, gathering its own water, and processing its own waste.

The home is divided into two pavilions, with one for living and one for sleeping. The construction was designed to give the versatility of construction either on site, or offsite (and transportable.)

The material palette is natural, and in tune with the environment. Exterior cladding is a heritage red Colorsteel, and the interior is almost completely timber.
