Our Process



Preliminary

● Site inspection and environmental analysis.
● Site measure existing buildings, if any (this is critical for existing use rights).
● Confirm client brief and budget.
● Arrange site survey / geotechnical consultants where required.


Concept Design

● Freehand or computer sketches of layout and appearance.
● 3D renderings of building aesthetics.


Developed Design

● Precise floor plans and elevations.
● Preliminary construction sections.
● Engage specialist consultants ir required (structural engineer, landscape architect).
● Apply for planning approval from council, ir required/desired.
● Obtain preliminary cost estimate from quantity surveyore or builder.


Detailed Documentation

● Prepare comprehensive drawings and specifications for building consent and construction tender.
● Design built in joinery items.
● Design bathroom layouts / select fixtures.
● Interior design services can include paint colours and soft furnishings.


Procurement

● Tender the project to select the builder
● Prepare the contract, including confirmation of insurances and licenses


Contract Administration /
Construction Observation

● Update documentation to include any changes due to building consent requirements.
● Confirm builders prices for any variations ahead of authorising payments.
● Check on construction progress, and confirm payment claims.
● Confirm practical completion, and issue defect remedials
